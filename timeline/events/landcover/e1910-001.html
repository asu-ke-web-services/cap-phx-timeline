Phoenix has 329 licensed automobiles. Wealthy residents form a committee to pave the streets. Many minority neighborhoods south of downtown are kept outside of the city’s official boundaries. They won’t have paved roads for a long time (Horton 1941, 30; De Potter Lykes in Johnson Jr., G.W. 1993 p 223).

Trees are planted on 15,000 acres across the Valley (Horton 1941 p 31).
