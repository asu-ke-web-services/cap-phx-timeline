1867

LAND CLAIMED BY MINERS AND SOLDIERS

Miners from Wickenburg and soldiers from Camp McDowell file claims on "the north bank of the Salt River near McDowell Crossing, in the eastern portion of the present Salt River Indian Reservation" for farming and milling purposes (Mawn 1979, 209).

1870

Selection of Phoenix townsite.

1871

Phoenix is voted as Maricopa County seat.
