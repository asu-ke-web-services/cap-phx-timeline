1999

In November, local residents march to protest the expansion of one hazardous waste facility, Innovative Waste Utility (IWU).

2000

In February, participants in the Rally to End Environmental Racism march on the state Capitol.

2004

In May 2004, Central Garden & Pet settles a class-action lawsuit with South Phoenix residents claiming health damages from the fire. The company pays out just less than $8 million (Arizona Republic, 2010).
