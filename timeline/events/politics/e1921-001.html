Medical professionals link poor nutrition to high rates of illness in South Phoenix. During this decade, death rates in Phoenix are two- to three- times higher for minorities than for Whites (Bolin et al. 2005, p.161 citing Buck 1936). More about structural injustices.

Phoenix Union Colored High School opens in 1926.

The Catholic Church becomes more segregated as Mexicans parishioners are forced by church officials to relocate and worship separately at the Immaculate Heart of Mary (Oberle and Arreola 2008).
