A federal housing agency, called the Home Owner’s Loan Corporation, begins “redlining” practices. It marks minority neighborhoods in red on maps to show that no financing should be granted to potential homeowners in those neighborhoods. As a result, families cannot buy homes in minority communities and the rental market becomes dominant.
