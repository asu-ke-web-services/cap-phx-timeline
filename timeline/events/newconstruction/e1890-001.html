The growing railroad network encourages Anglo immigration to Phoenix (Oberle and Arreola 2008, 173 citing C. Sargent 1988 and Dean and Reynolds 2006, 13).

Residents complain the city needs more paved roads and sidewalks (De Potter Lykes in Johnson Jr., G.W. 1993 p 222).
