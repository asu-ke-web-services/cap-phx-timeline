McCall Cotton and Oil Company at 7th and Grant streets increases production (Bolin et al. 2005, p. 162 citing Mawn 1979).

Phoenix is now a major hub for shipping petroleum and agricultural products to the Southwest. In 1925, a Shell jobbership opens on 15th Avenue. The plant will eventually become Western States Petroleum, which will still be operating in 2012.
