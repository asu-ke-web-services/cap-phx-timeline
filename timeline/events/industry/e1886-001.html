1886

Electric plant and steam engines that burned cordwood established (Horton, 1941, pp 24).

1887

Mule driven street railway operated by Valley Street Railway Company (Horton 1941 pp 24).

The Southern Pacific Railroad is built in 1887. It runs east to west, forming what will become a clear line between North and South Phoenix. Like most railroads, this spurs industrial development in South Phoenix (Luckingham 1994; Bolin et al. 2005).
